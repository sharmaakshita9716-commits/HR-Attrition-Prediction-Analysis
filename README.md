This Project addresses the high cost of employee turnover by combining Machine learning(Python) with Business Intelligence (Power BI). By analyzing historical workforce data, I developed a model to predict which employees are at risk of leaving , identify a potential $20.4M impact on the organization. 
Tech Stack: Python(Pandas,Numpy),Scikit-learn(Random Forest/Logistic Regression),PowerBI desktop,Google colab.
Key insights:
Financial Risk: The "Left" segment represents a calculated replacement cost of $20M.
High-Risk Department: Sales shows the highest average risk score at 76.12,followed closely by HR and R&D.
Predictive Accuracy: Transitioned from raw 0/1 data to a Weighted Risk Score(0-100) to help HR prioritize "Stay interviews".
